Upping The Coolness Quotient Quite Literally: Cars With Dual Zone Climate Control Under Rs 30 Lakh -

Cars today are different from what they were a few decades ago. They are no longer mere means to travel but are also one of the places where we spend most of our time. So to enhance the in-cabin experience for occupants, carmakers have been making them more loaded than ever before, especially from mass-and Kia are a few his nifty bit of creature comfort:

2023 Kia Seltos -

Once launched, the new Seltos will become the first compact SUV in India to get dual-zone climate control. Kia is offering this luxury feature from the higher-spec HTX onwards for the updated SUV. It will also be the most affordable car in the market to get dual-zone AC, possibly until the facelifted Hyundai Creta arrives.

Toyota Innova Hycross -

When Toyota launched the third-gen Innova towards the end of 2022 also known as the Innova HyCross it surprised everyone with some unique characteristics of the MPV and premium feature additions. One such inclusion was of the dual-zone climate control, although it is reserved for the top- spec ZX and ZX (O) Hybrid variants. Unlike other models on this list which of fer dual-zone AC for the two individual front occupants, the convenience feat ure on the Innova Hycross is rather split between the front and second row passe ngers.
